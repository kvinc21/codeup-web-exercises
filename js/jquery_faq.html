National Parks FAQ

What do you know about National Parks?
They were founded by an Act on March 1, 1872.
How many National Parks are there?
The National Park System encompasses 423 national park sites in the United States
How many acres are there in the National Park System?
There are 84 million acres of land.
Are the National Parks on the main land of the United States?
No, there are parks in Puerto Rico, the Virgin Islands, American Samoa and Guam?
